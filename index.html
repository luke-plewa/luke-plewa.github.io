My two requirements for working on a project are that I am able to create something I am proud of and that I have fun doing it. A great example would be my work with Verdigris Technologies to develop Building.AI. Not only is the work environment awesome, but the project (energy intelligence) is one I enjoy telling others about. By reducing the carbon footprint of office space buildings, I'm proud of myself by making a contribution to the world that will outlive me. My upcoming Master's Thesis focuses on improving the state of sudden cardiac arrest prediction through Electrocardiograms and Heart Rate Variability. The current techniques were built upon small datasets. I strive to improve accuracy in prediction while demonstrating that my model will be able to adapt to new, inevitable datasets. Bringing data science into the medical field will be important with the capabilities of wearable tech and may help us reach out to undeveloped areas at lower costs.
